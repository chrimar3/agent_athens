On December 5th and 6th, Italian doom metal experimentalists MESSA bring their haunting sonic landscapes to Greece for two rare performances across multiple venues. This touring appearance represents significant opportunity for Greek metal audiences to experience one of Europe's most distinctive heavy music projects, a band that has spent the past decade expanding doom metal's vocabulary while remaining deeply committed to the genre's core values of weight, atmosphere, and emotional intensity.

MESSA emerged from Italy's vibrant underground metal scene in 2014, quickly distinguishing themselves through an approach that honors doom metal's traditions while incorporating elements typically foreign to the genre. Their music combines crushing heaviness with jazz-influenced saxophone, liturgical aesthetics with psychedelic experimentation, and traditional doom structures with progressive song development.
