On Saturday, December 6th, 2025, adventure seekers and nature enthusiasts have the opportunity to escape Athens' urban sprawl for an enriching day trip to three remarkable destinations in the Peloponnese: Agia Fotini, Kapsia Cave, and Lagkadia village. Departing at 7:30 AM from Metro Larisis Station, this carefully curated excursion combines spiritual heritage, geological wonder, and traditional mountain culture into one memorable experience.

For those interested in geology, traditional architecture, Orthodox spirituality, or simply escaping the city for pristine mountain air and stunning landscapes, this December day trip offers exceptional value and memorable experiences in one of Greece's most beautiful and historically significant regions.
