Musical Narrations presents The Rooster's Troubles at Polyxoros Politismou Diefsis on Sunday November 9th at noon, adapting a Far Eastern folktale by Kostas Fotinos that teaches how strength becomes weakness and weakness strength. This story explores how different people perceive happiness and what truly matters in life, creating accessible philosophy for audiences aged 5 to 105 through the combination of live music and storytelling that defines Musical Narrations' decade-long approach to bringing folklore to contemporary audiences.

For families seeking cultural experiences introducing children to wisdom traditions beyond contemporary commercial entertainment, The Rooster's Troubles offers engaging entry point into folklore's rich heritage. The combination of music and narration creates multisensory experience supporting different learning styles and attention patterns. The age range from 5 to 105 acknowledges that good stories transcend demographic categories when told well, creating shared experiences across generational divides where children and adults find different meanings in the same narrative.
