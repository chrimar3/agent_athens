While the remix brought commercial success, it only hinted at Avidan's actual artistry—his original work operates in entirely different territory: raw, vulnerable, literary, and emotionally uncompromising. His solo career, launched after the band's dissolution, has revealed an artist uninterested in repeating commercial formulas, instead following his artistic instincts wherever they lead.
