The Gazarte Roof Stage provides the perfect backdrop for this winter series. This 200-capacity venue sits atop one of Athens' most beloved cultural complexes at Pireos 100, offering sweeping views of the city skyline that transform as evening descends over the Gazi neighborhood. The rooftop setting creates an almost cinematic experience, where Manousaki's music floats above the vibrant streets below, merging with the urban energy of one of Athens' most dynamic districts.

Gazi and Keramikos have long been at the forefront of Athens' cultural renaissance. Once an industrial area dominated by the historic gasworks, this neighborhood has evolved into the city's creative heart, where converted warehouses host galleries, music venues, and performance spaces. Gazarte itself exemplifies this transformation, occupying multiple levels of a restored industrial building and offering everything from intimate concerts to art exhibitions. The venue's proximity to Kerameikos metro station makes it easily accessible, while its position in the neighborhood's cultural corridor means concertgoers can extend their evening exploring nearby bars, restaurants, and venues.

For this winter series, Manousaki has assembled a band of excellent musicians whose collective talent promises to elevate her already impressive performances. At Gazarte Roof Stage on November 7th, audiences will witness not just a concert, but the continuation of Sophia Manousaki's remarkable artistic journey.
