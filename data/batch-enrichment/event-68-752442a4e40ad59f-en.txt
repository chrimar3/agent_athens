On November 12, 2025, Stavros tou Notou Club in the vibrant Neos Kosmos neighborhood presents "Epilogos" (Epilogue) by Alexandros Gourgoullios, featuring the exceptional talents of Vasilis Prodromou and Mariana Katsimichas. This intimate concert promises an evening of refined Greek music in one of Athens' most beloved cultural venues.

Stavros tou Notou Club has established itself as a cornerstone of Athens' contemporary music scene, offering an intimate setting with a capacity of 200 that creates an unparalleled connection between performers and audience. Located in Neos Kosmos, a neighborhood that beautifully balances residential charm with cultural vibrancy, the venue embodies the spirit of modern Athens while maintaining deep roots in Greek musical tradition. The club's reputation for hosting high-quality performances makes it the perfect stage for Gourgoullios' latest work.

Alexandros Gourgoullios has carved a distinctive place in contemporary Greek music, known for compositions that bridge traditional elements with modern sensibilities. "Epilogos" represents a significant artistic statement, suggesting themes of conclusion, reflection, and perhaps new beginnings. The title itself invites audiences to contemplate the cyclical nature of artistic creation and the meaningful closure that allows for future growth.

Vasilis Prodromou brings his considerable musical expertise to this performance, adding depth and nuance to Gourgoullios' compositions. His collaboration with Mariana Katsimichas promises a dynamic interplay of voices and interpretations that will illuminate different facets of the work. Katsimichas, recognized for her expressive vocal abilities and emotional range, adds a complementary dimension to the evening's artistic vision.

The intimate atmosphere of Stavros tou Notou Club enhances the experience, allowing every subtle musical gesture and lyrical phrase to resonate with clarity. The venue's carefully considered acoustics and thoughtful design create an environment where music becomes a shared experience rather than mere entertainment. In a space that holds only 200 people, each audience member becomes part of the performance's energy.

For those seeking authentic Greek cultural experiences beyond tourist-oriented venues, this concert represents Athens at its artistic best. The combination of Gourgoullios' compositional vision, the performing talents of Prodromou and Katsimichas, and the unique ambiance of Stavros tou Notou Club creates an evening that speaks to both tradition and innovation. This is Greek music performed by Greeks for audiences who appreciate artistry, presented in a neighborhood venue that embodies the city's living cultural landscape.

The November 12 performance offers a rare opportunity to witness contemporary Greek music in its most authentic setting, where artistic integrity and audience connection remain paramount.
