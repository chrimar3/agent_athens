On November 30th, 2025, Kyttaro Live in Athens' Exarchia neighborhood hosts a triple-bill hardcore showcase featuring Medusa's Wrath, Dentures, and Daisy on a Shelf. Medusa's Wrath headlines this showcase, bringing their particular brand of aggressive hardcore that balances technical precision with raw emotional power. The band represents the latest evolution of Athens' hardcore scene, which has maintained continuity since the 1990s while constantly renewing itself through new bands who honor the genre's history without being constrained by it. Their music channels hardcore's traditional elements—breakneck tempos, shouted vocals, politically-charged lyrics—while incorporating metallic heaviness and rhythmic complexity that speaks to listeners raised on diverse musical influences.

Dentures and Daisy on a Shelf round out the lineup, each bringing distinct approaches to hardcore's broad possibilities. The diversity within this single bill demonstrates hardcore's capacity to encompass everything from straightforward punk aggression to experimental boundary-pushing, all unified by commitment to intensity and authenticity. The Exarchia location carries particular significance for hardcore punk, a genre historically aligned with leftist politics, anti-authoritarianism, and community self-organization. The neighborhood's resistance to police presence, its history of squats and autonomous social centers, and its continuing role as Athens' countercultural hub make it ideologically aligned with hardcore's founding principles. Playing Kyttaro isn't just booking a venue—it's participating in a cultural ecosystem where music, politics, and community organizing intersect.
