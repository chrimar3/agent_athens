On November 13, 2025, at 21:00, MEGARON - The Athens Concert Hall welcomes one of contemporary jazz's most celebrated voices: Stacey Kent and her exceptional trio. This performance marks a rare opportunity to experience the Grammy-nominated vocalist in Athens' most prestigious concert venue, a 2000-capacity architectural landmark nestled in the elegant Kolonaki and Ilissia districts.

Stacey Kent has captivated audiences worldwide with her intimate, sophisticated interpretations of the Great American Songbook and Brazilian bossa nova standards. Her crystalline voice and impeccable phrasing have earned her multiple platinum albums and collaborations with legendary composers including Nobel laureate Kazuo Ishiguro, who penned original lyrics for her recordings. Kent's artistry transcends typical jazz performance; she creates an atmosphere of warmth and sophistication that transforms each concert into an unforgettable experience.

The trio format showcases Kent at her most refined. Accompanied by world-class musicians who have toured extensively across Europe, North America, and Asia, the ensemble delivers arrangements that balance technical brilliance with emotional depth. Expect timeless standards reimagined through Kent's unique lens, alongside carefully selected contemporary compositions that highlight her versatility as an interpreter and storyteller.

MEGARON provides the perfect acoustic setting for Kent's nuanced vocal style. Located in the cultural heart of Athens, where Kolonaki's refined elegance meets Ilissia's leafy boulevards, the concert hall has hosted the world's finest performers since its inauguration. The venue's exceptional acoustics ensure that every subtle inflection and harmonic detail reaches the audience with clarity and presence, making it Athens' premier destination for discerning music lovers.
