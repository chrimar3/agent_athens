When Alexandros Daikos takes the stage at Kyttaro Live on November 27th, audiences will witness one of Greece's most innovative contemporary artists blending traditional sounds with modern experimental rhythms. Known for his project Otra Rota, Daikos has carved out a distinctive space in Athens' vibrant music scene, creating soundscapes that defy easy categorization while remaining deeply rooted in Mediterranean musical traditions.

Otra Rota represents Daikos' exploration of cultural fusion, weaving together elements from Greek folk music, Latin American rhythms, and contemporary electronic production. The name itself, playing on Spanish words meaning "another route," perfectly captures the artist's approach to breaking conventional boundaries. His performances are known for their improvisational energy, where structured compositions give way to spontaneous musical conversations that respond to the mood and energy of each unique audience.

Kyttaro Live provides the perfect setting for this sonic journey. Located in the culturally diverse neighborhood of Metaxourgeio, near Viktoria Square, this intimate 500-capacity venue has become synonymous with quality live music in Athens. The venue's raw industrial aesthetic, with exposed brick walls and minimal ornamentation, creates an atmosphere where music takes center stage without distraction. Metaxourgeio itself has transformed in recent years from an overlooked district into one of Athens' most dynamic cultural quarters, home to galleries, alternative theater spaces, and a thriving nightlife scene that attracts both locals and visitors seeking authentic experiences beyond the tourist trail.
