This First Aid workshop covers fundamental emergency response techniques that every adult should master. Participants will learn cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR), how to manage choking incidents, control bleeding, treat burns, and respond to common medical emergencies. The training emphasizes practical application through realistic scenarios, ensuring that attendees don't just understand the theory but can actually perform these critical interventions when seconds count.
