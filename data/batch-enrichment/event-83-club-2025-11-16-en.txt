With a capacity of 200, the club offers an intimate setting where the connection between performer and audience becomes palpable. The space has been carefully designed to prioritize acoustics and atmosphere, creating an environment where music can breathe and resonate with clarity. This is not a venue for passive listening but rather a gathering place for those who seek genuine musical experiences.

The Neos Kosmos neighborhood itself adds another layer to the evening's appeal. This area, positioned between the Acropolis Museum and Syngrou-Fix metro station, represents a fascinating blend of residential Athens and cultural vitality. The streets surrounding Stavros tou Notou are lined with traditional tavernas, modern cafes, and local shops, making the area perfect for pre-concert exploration. The neighborhood's authentic character provides a grounding context for the evening's performance, reminding visitors that Athens' cultural life extends far beyond the tourist-heavy zones of Plaka and Monastiraki.
