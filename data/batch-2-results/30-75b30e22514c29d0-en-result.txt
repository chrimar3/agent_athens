This evening performance begins at 20:00, offering Athens theater enthusiasts an opportunity to engage with powerful storytelling in one of the city's most culturally significant venues.

The Tzeni Karezi Theater, named after one of Greece's most beloved actresses, stands as a monument to theatrical excellence in the heart of Athens. This venue has hosted countless productions that have shaped modern Greek theater, making it the perfect setting for a performance centered on female experience and transformation. The theater's intimate atmosphere allows audiences to connect deeply with performances, creating an immersive experience that resonates long after the final curtain.

"Struggles and Metamorphoses of a Woman" promises to be a thought-provoking exploration of the universal themes of personal growth, societal expectations, and the ongoing evolution of female identity.
